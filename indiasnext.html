OUR STORY

India

India’s Next 40 is Ycenter’s flagship entrepreneurship focussed program in India. The goal of the

program is to use our creative problem solving methodology to help the youth of India create;

meaningful, human centered businesses.



Participants in the last India's Next 40 program giving feedback to each other.



India has one of the largest youth population in the world. Youth in India are gravitating more and

more towards the startup world, which has also received a massive impetus in the last couple of

years. But as is often the case, a lot of startups are created following fads, or are not well thought

out enough to last more than a year in the market.


India’s Next 40 provides an opportunity to these youth to explore their dreams, solve meaningful

problems, and become more empathetic through the journey. Participants learn in small cohorts,

helping each other progress by providing feedback and encouragement. Ycenter brings together

industry specific experts & investors to help the participants transition from having a vague idea to

start with, building it into something specific and then taking it into the real world outside the classroom.


Vision Builder has been running the India’s Next 40 program for the last two years and we have had many

success stories. Be it a startup that are launched and functioning, or giving somebody the nudge

they need to seriously consider spending time to build their dreams into reality.


Mock interviews session in class.


The goal of the programs was to engage youth ,through our experiential learning pedagogy, to work

on agricultural challenges. Over the two programs we had several promising projects, some of

which are being supported by the University of Nairobi.
